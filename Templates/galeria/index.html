About

Our molecular database is a tool designed to advance the struggle against leishmaniasis, a neglected tropical disease that impacts more than 350 million people globally. In this sense, this project aims to make a vast collection of molecules available, allowing users to explore, visualize and compare data based on Tanimoto similarity. We understand the urgency in the search for new therapeutic molecules, given the limitations, toxicity and risks of resistance of existing treatments.
